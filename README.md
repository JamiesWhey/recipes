## 🍳 Breakfast Sandwich Situation

A structured, build-your-own breakfast sandwich designed for real cravings and real life. Toasted keto or low-carb bread, melted measured cheese, savory protein, and a fresh or convenient egg — simple, repeatable, satisfying.

⸻  
🔧 The Exact Build  

Protein — (Choose ONE)  
• 4–6 oz turkey bacon  
• 4–6 oz turkey sausage  
• 4–6 oz Canadian bacon  
• 4–6 oz lean deli ham  
• 4–6 oz pork tenderloin  
• 4–6 oz Jamie’s Fried Chicken  

Protein is the anchor. Build your sandwich around it.

⸻  
Egg  
• 1–2 whole large eggs  

⸻  
Bread — 1 Serving (Keto or Low-Carb)  
Choose ONE:  
• 2 slices keto or low-carb bread  
• 1 keto or low-carb sandwich thin  
• 1 low-carb English muffin  
• 1 keto-style bagel  
• 1 low-carb wrap (folded and toasted)  

⸻  
Cheese (Jamie’s Rule — Exact Amount)  
• 1/6 cup reduced-fat shredded cheese  

Measured. Never free-poured.

⸻  
🔥 Assembly  

Cook protein fully.  
Toast bread and add measured cheese while toasting so it melts into the surface.  
Cook egg to preference.  
Assemble: cheese-melted bread, protein, egg, top bread.

## 👑 Maple Sausage Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients  
• 4 oz 96/4 lean ground beef  
• 2 oz 90/10 ground pork  
• ½–¾ tsp maple seasoning  
• ¼ tsp salt  
• Black pepper to taste  
• 1 whole large egg  
• 1 keto or low-carb sandwich thin  
• 1/6 cup reduced-fat shredded cheese  

Instructions  

Combine beef, pork, maple seasoning, salt, and pepper.  
Form into a thin patty.  
Cook over medium heat until browned and fully cooked.  
Toast bread and sprinkle measured cheese on top while toasting so it melts into the surface.  
Cook egg to preference.  
Assemble: cheese-melted bread, sausage patty, egg, top bread.

## 🥞🧇 Pancake & Waffle Situation

A structured batter base designed for pancakes or waffles. Greek yogurt improves texture, moisture, and flavor while protein additions anchor the meal. Crisp edges. Soft center. Repeatable.

Pancakes and waffles share the same batter — only the cooking method changes.

⸻  
🔧 Keto Flour-Based Batter (With Greek Yogurt)

Exact Build  
• ½ cup King Arthur pancake mix  
• ½ scoop approved protein powder  
• 1 whole egg  
• 2–3 tbsp vanilla high-protein Greek yogurt  

Liquid (Choose ONE)  
• 1 cup ready-to-drink protein shake  
OR  
• 1 cup unsweetened almond or coconut milk  

Mix until thick but pourable. Adjust liquid if needed.

⸻  
🔧 Cottage Cheese Protein Batter  

• 1 cup cottage cheese  
• 2 whole eggs  
• ½ scoop approved protein powder  
• 2–3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt  
• ⅛ tsp baking powder  

Blend until smooth.

⸻  
🔥 Cooking  

• Skillet over medium heat (pancakes)  
• Waffle iron until crisp (waffles)

⸻

## 👑 Jamie’s Whey – Lemon Blueberry Coconut Faux’ts

• 1–1½ cups vanilla high-protein yogurt  
• ½ scoop protein powder  
• 1 tablespoon lemon sugar-free pudding mix  
• ½ cup frozen blueberries  
• 2 tablespoons unsweetened shredded coconut  
• ¼ cup blueberry protein cereal  

Mix thoroughly.  
Refrigerate overnight.  
Serve cold.
